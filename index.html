About Us

HybriMoto is a next-generation mobility startup focused on designing and building smart, eco-efficient Hybrid Motorcycles from the ground up. Our mission is to deliver a cost-effective, dual-powered two-wheeler that combines the strength of Internal Combustion with the precision of electric mobility‚Äîperfectly suited for urban and semi-urban consumers in India.

Our Products

We are making an AWD (All-wheel Drive) Hybrid Motorcycle powered by twin motors and an IC engine. Our hybrid system includes four modes: ICE, EV, Assist, and Smart. The Smart mode automatically optimizes performance using driving conditions.
