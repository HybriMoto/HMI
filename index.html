About Us

HybriMoto is a next-generation mobility startup focused on designing and building smart, eco-efficient Hybrid Motorcycles from the ground up. Our mission is to deliver a cost-effective, dual-powered two-wheeler that combines the strength of Internal Combustion with the precision of electric mobility‚Äîperfectly suited for urban and semi-urban consumers in India. We aim to make sustainable transportation both accessible and affordable for everyday riders.

Our Products

We are making an AWD (All-wheel Drive) Hybrid Motorcycle that is powered by twin motors and an IC engine. Our hybrid vehicle features four distinct operating modes: ICE, EV, Assist, and Smart. A manual switch allows users to seamlessly toggle between ICE, EV, and Assist modes based on driving needs. The Smart mode automatically selects the optimal mode by analyzing driving conditions, ensuring maximum efficiency and performance without driver intervention.
